# Building the Open Ecosystem Platform for Strong Agents

## A Common Platform for Infrastructure, Skills, Benchmarks, Applications, and Community Growth

We are not building a single workflow, a single graph runtime, or a single framework.

We are building an open ecosystem platform for the strong-agent era: a shared platform layer that can support reusable skills, open benchmarks, domain applications, and new forms of agent-native products, research, and collaboration.

### Platform Scope

- A common **platform foundation** for controllers, operators, protocols, runtime, state, recovery, and graph execution
- A reusable **skill platform** where capabilities can be authored, loaded, benchmarked, shared, composed, and eventually exchanged
- A broad **application platform** spanning auto-research, reliable agentic systems, agentic RL, and domain-specific workflows
- An open **ecosystem platform** for benchmarks, leaderboards, community contributions, institutional collaboration, and future marketplaces

### What This Means

- Strong agents are becoming the new execution primitive
- The missing piece is not another prompt wrapper, but the platform around them: infrastructure, skills, evaluation, and distribution
- The graph runtime is important, but it is only one subsystem inside a much larger ecosystem platform

### One-Line Positioning

**Build the open ecosystem platform for the strong-agent era.**
